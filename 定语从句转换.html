英语定语从句练习系统

通过以下练习，掌握定语从句的拆分与合并技巧

第一部分：将包含定语从句的复句改写为两个单句

1. The book which I borrowed from the library is very interesting.

2. The man who is standing over there is my uncle.

3. The house that we bought last year is very spacious.

4. The girl whose brother is a doctor studies at our school.

5. The movie that we watched yesterday was thrilling.

6. The teacher who teaches us English is very kind.

7. The car which is parked outside belongs to my neighbor.

8. The student whose homework was perfect got an award.

9. The city where I was born is very beautiful.

10. The day when we met was unforgettable.

第二部分：将两个单句改写为一个包含定语从句的复句

1. The book is on the table. I bought it yesterday.

2. The man is my teacher. He is standing by the window.

3. The house is very old. We live in it.

4. The girl is my friend. Her father is a pilot.

5. The movie was exciting. We saw it last night.

6. The teacher is very experienced. She teaches us math.

7. The car is red. It is parked in the garage.

8. The boy won the competition. His essay was outstanding.

9. The city is famous. I was born there.

10. The day was special. We got married then.
