宾语从句与简单句转换练习

通过将宾语从句复合句转换为简单句，或将简单句合并为宾语从句复合句，提升语法转换能力。难度约雅思7分水平。

第一部分：将宾语从句复合句转换为两个简单句

请将以下包含宾语从句的复合句改写为两个简单句。输入答案后点击“Submit”查看标准答案。

1. The professor explained that renewable energy could reduce carbon emissions.

标准答案: The professor explained something. Renewable energy could reduce carbon emissions.

2. She asked whether the new policy would improve productivity.

标准答案: She asked something. The new policy would improve productivity.

3. The manager wondered how the team had achieved the sales target.

标准答案: The manager wondered something. The team had achieved the sales target.

4. Experts predicted that automation would transform industries by 2030.

标准答案: Experts predicted something. Automation would transform industries by 2030.

5. The report revealed what factors had caused the economic downturn.

标准答案: The report revealed something. Factors had caused the economic downturn.

6. He admitted that he had underestimated the project’s complexity.

标准答案: He admitted something. He had underestimated the project’s complexity.

7. The team discussed whether remote work would remain popular.

标准答案: The team discussed something. Remote work would remain popular.

8. She questioned why the company had delayed the product launch.

标准答案: She questioned something. The company had delayed the product launch.

9. Analysts believed that sustainable practices enhanced brand value.

标准答案: Analysts believed something. Sustainable practices enhanced brand value.

10. The survey indicated what groups benefited most from the policy.

标准答案: The survey indicated something. Groups benefited most from the policy.

第二部分：将两个简单句合并为包含宾语从句的复合句

请将以下两个简单句合并为一个包含宾语从句的复合句。可能需要适当修改或删减句子成分以使句子自然流畅。输入答案后点击“Submit”查看标准答案。

11. What happened to the missing hikers? The rescue team was curious about the situation.

标准答案: The rescue team was curious about what happened to the missing hikers.

12. My phone is broken. I complained to the store manager.

标准答案: I complained to the store manager that my phone was broken.

13. How can I improve my English speaking skills? I asked my teacher for advice.

标准答案: I asked my teacher how I could improve my English speaking skills.

14. Will the concert be canceled? I was wondering about the weather forecast.

标准答案: I was wondering whether the concert would be canceled.

15. The train is delayed. The station staff informed passengers.

标准答案: The station staff informed passengers that the train was delayed.

16. Why are the prices so high? The customer questioned the shop assistant.

标准答案: The customer questioned the shop assistant why the prices were so high.

17. Healthy eating improves energy levels. My coach explained the benefits.

标准答案: My coach explained that healthy eating improves energy levels.

18. Who won the game last night? My brother wanted to know the final score.

标准答案: My brother wanted to know who won the game last night.

19. I lost my wallet. I told the police officer about the incident.

标准答案: I told the police officer that I had lost my wallet.

20. Where is the nearest bus stop? The tourist asked a local for directions.

标准答案: The tourist asked a local where the nearest bus stop was.
