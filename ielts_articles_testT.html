IELTS Writing Task 1: Articles Practice Test with Answers

Instructions

Below are the same 20 questions from the IELTS Writing Task 1 Articles Practice Test, with the correct articles ('a', 'an', 'the', or no article '/') filled in. Each question is followed by an explanation of why the answers are correct, designed to reflect typical IELTS Writing Task 1 contexts for describing charts, graphs, and tables.

1. The diagram depicts the process of the water cycle in nature.

Correct: The, the, the

Explanation: 'Diagram' is specific (the one described), so 'the'; 'process' is specific in context, so 'the'; 'water cycle' is unique, so 'the'.

2. The figures reveal a gradual decline in the sales of / newspapers over the period.

Correct: The, a, the, /, the

Explanation: 'Figures' is specific (in data), so 'the'; 'decline' is one of many, consonant sound, so 'a'; 'sales' is specific, so 'the'; 'newspapers' is plural and general, no article; 'period' is specific (in data), so 'the'.

3. In 2019, the expenditure on / education in the United States was the highest among the countries surveyed.

Correct: the, /, the, the, the

Explanation: 'Expenditure' is specific (in 2019), so 'the'; 'education' is uncountable and general, no article; 'United States' is a proper noun with 'the'; 'highest' is superlative, so 'the'; 'countries' is specific (surveyed), so 'the'.

4. The map shows the changes in the layout of the town from 1990 to 2020.

Correct: The, the, the, the

Explanation: 'Map' is specific, so 'the'; 'changes' is specific (in data), so 'the'; 'layout' is specific, so 'the'; 'town' is specific (in context), so 'the'.

5. The proportion of the workforce employed in / agriculture decreased significantly.

Correct: The, the, /

Explanation: 'Proportion' is specific (in data), so 'the'; 'workforce' is specific (in context), so 'the'; 'agriculture' is uncountable and general, no article.

6. The information provided indicates a peak in / tourism during the summer months.

7. The line chart compares the levels of / pollution in three cities.

8. The overall trend shows an increase in the adoption of / renewable sources.

Explanation: 'Overall trend' is specific, so 'the'; 'increase' is one of many, vowel sound, so 'an'; 'adoption' is specific, so 'the'; 'renewable sources' is plural and general, no article.
