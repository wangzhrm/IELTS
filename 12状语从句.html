状语从句知识梳理

什么是状语从句？

状语从句在复合句中作状语，修饰动词、形容词或副词等。状语从句可以表示时间、条件、原因、地点、目的、结果、让步、方式、比较等意义。

Whenever he comes, he brings a friend. (时间状语从句)

Put it where we can all see it. (地点状语从句)

Though he is in poor health, he works hard. (让步状语从句)

状语从句分类及连接词

类型 连接词/短语 例句
时间 when, while, as, as soon as, since, until, after, before I will call you when I arrive.
条件 if, unless, as long as If it rains, we will stay home.
原因 as, because, since He didn't come because he was sick.
地点 where, wherever Put it where we can see it.
目的 so that, in order that She studies hard so that she can pass the exam.
结果 so that, so...that, such...that It was so hot that we couldn't sleep.
让步 though, although, even if, however Although it was late, we continued working.
方式 as, as if, as though Do as I told you.
比较 than, (not) as...as She is taller than her brother.

易混淆连接词对比

when, while, as 的区别

when - 既可以指"时间点"，也可以指"时间段"

When he came in, his mother was cooking.

while - 表示时间段，谓语动词要用延续性动词

Please don't talk so loud while others are working.

as - 强调主句动作与从句动作同时发生

As you get older, you get more knowledge.

because, as, since 的区别

because - 表示直接原因，回答why问题，语气最强

Water is very important because we can't live without it.

as - 说明原因，着重点在主句，常译成"由于"

He didn't come yesterday as his mother was ill.

since - 表示显然的或已知的理由或事实，常译成"既然"

I'll do it for you since you are busy.

结果状语从句结构对比

such...that - such是形容词，修饰名词

This was such a good film that I went to see it several times.

It was such bad weather that I had to stay at home.

so...that - so是副词，修饰形容词或副词

He spoke so fast that I couldn't follow him.

She made so many mistakes that she didn't pass the exam.

转换结构

He is such a lovely boy that we all like him.

= He is so lovely a boy that we all like him.

状语从句练习

1 ______ he comes, we will start the meeting.
A As soon as
B Because
C So that
D Though
正确答案与解析
正确答案是 A. As soon as。
解析：此句需要时间状语从句，表示"他一来我们就开始会议"。As soon as 表示"一...就..."，符合句意。
2 I will lend you the book ______ you return it on time.
A as if
B so that
C as long as
D even if
正确答案与解析
正确答案是 C. as long as。
解析：此句需要条件状语从句，表示"只要你按时归还"。as long as 表示条件"只要"，符合句意。
3 ______ it was raining, we still went out.
A Because
B Since
C Although
D As
正确答案与解析
正确答案是 C. Although。
解析：此句需要让步状语从句，表示"尽管下雨，我们还是出去了"。Although 表示"尽管"，符合句意。
4 She is ______ a good student ______ everyone likes her.
A such...that
B so...that
C as...as
D too...to
正确答案与解析
正确答案是 A. such...that。
解析：such修饰名词短语"a good student"，that引导结果状语从句，表示"她是如此好的学生以至于每个人都喜欢她"。
5 He spoke ______ fast ______ I couldn't understand him.
A such...that
B so...that
C as...as
D so...as
正确答案与解析
正确答案是 B. so...that。
解析：so修饰副词"fast"，that引导结果状语从句，表示"他说得如此快以至于我听不懂他"。
