英语表语从句改写练习

基于表语从句知识，练习句子改写。难度相当于雅思7分水平。

第一部分：将包含表语从句的复合句改写为两个单句

对于每道题，将给定的复合句拆分为两个独立的单句。第一个单句应保留主句结构，并用"this"或适当词语替换表语从句。第二个单句应是原表语从句的内容。

  1. The problem is that many endangered species are facing extinction due to rampant habitat destruction.
  2. It seems as though technological innovations are outpacing our ability to regulate them ethically.
  3. The reason for the economic downturn is that global supply chains have been severely disrupted by geopolitical tensions.
  4. My primary concern is whether artificial intelligence will exacerbate social inequalities in the long term.
  5. It appears that climate change is contributing to more unpredictable weather patterns worldwide.
  6. The truth is that social media platforms often amplify misinformation during critical events.
  7. The central issue is how governments can balance fiscal responsibility with investments in renewable energy.
  8. It looks as if international trade agreements are on the verge of collapse amid rising protectionism.
  9. The lingering question is why cultural diversity is essential for fostering innovation in modern societies.
  10. That is what renders sustainable agriculture so challenging in arid regions.

第二部分：将两个单句改写为包含表语从句的复合句

对于每道题，将给定的两个单句合并为一个包含表语从句的复合句。使用适当的连接词（如that, whether, as if, how等）来引导表语从句。

  1. The fact remains this. Urbanization is accelerating at an unprecedented rate in developing countries.
  2. It feels this way. Globalization has interconnected economies more than ever before.
  3. The reason was this. The project failed due to inadequate funding and poor planning.
  4. The dilemma is this. Should we invest in space exploration or address poverty on Earth first?
  5. It seems this. Renewable energy sources are becoming more cost-effective than fossil fuels.
  6. The core problem is this. How can we mitigate the impacts of overpopulation on natural resources?
  7. The truth is this. Mental health issues are often stigmatized in many cultures.
  8. It appears this way. Automation is transforming the job market irreversibly.
  9. The question is this. Why do some educational systems prioritize rote learning over critical thinking?
  10. That is this. What makes biodiversity conservation a global priority.
