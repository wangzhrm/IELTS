IELTS Writing Task 1: Articles Practice for Data Description

These 20 practice questions test your mastery of articles ('a', 'an', 'the', or no article '/') in sentences typical of IELTS Writing Task 1, where you describe data from charts, graphs, or tables. Each sentence has blanks for you to select the correct article. After choosing, click "Show Answer" to see the correct answers and a clear explanation. The sentences are designed to reflect common IELTS Writing Task 1 contexts, with unambiguous correct answers to avoid disputes.

1. ______ chart illustrates ______ significant increase in ______ global population from 1990 to 2020.

Correct: The , a , the
Explanation: 'Chart' is specific (the one being described), so 'the'; 'increase' is one of many possible, consonant sound, so 'a'; 'global population' is unique, so 'the'.
