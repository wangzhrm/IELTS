1. Strong and Direct (When you firmly agree or disagree)

I firmly believe that...
I strongly agree/disagree that...
I am convinced that...
It is my firm conviction that...
There is no doubt in my mind that...

2. Balanced or Partial Agreement

Generally, I partially agree with the perspective/idea/statement
While I acknowledge that..., I believe that... (Extremely useful and high-scoring)
Although it is true that..., my view is that...
Despite the arguments for..., I still favour...
On balance, I tend to believe that...
I largely agree with the idea that...; however,...

3. Neutral and Academic

I believe … has both its advantages and disadvantages (upsides and downsides/benefits and side effects).
This essay will argue that the above idea/perspective/statement has both its strengths and weaknesses
But I believe a more balanced approach may be more appropriate
Yet, I believe the validity of this argument depends on its context.
From my perspective, neither side can be regarded as completely correct or incorrect.
