动词知识梳理

一、动词分类

动词是表示动作（study, find, swim 等）或状态（be, like, feel 等）的词。动词具有人称、数量、时态、语态和语态变化。

动词分类： 动词有助动词、情态动词、联系动词和行为动词四类。

1. 助动词 (Auxiliary Verbs)

助动词没有任何含义，不能单独做谓语，只能和行为动词一起构成谓语，帮助行为动词组成各种时态、语态、语气以及疑问或否定形式。

助动词 用法 示例
be 构成进行时态、被动语态、表示计划安排 It was raining all day yesterday.
The meeting was held yesterday.
They are to see a film.
do 构成疑问句、否定句、表示强调 Does he think so?
I didn't say anything.
They do study hard.
have 构成完成时态 He has lived here for three years.
shall/should 构成将来时态 I shall send ten letters.
She asked if I should go.

2. 情态动词 (Modal Verbs)

情态动词本身有一定含义，但不能单独作谓语，要和行为动词一起构成谓语。

能力表示： can/could, be able to

Rose can speak now, but she couldn't a week ago.

She was able to finish the task last night.

许可表示： may/might, can/could

- Could I borrow your book?
- Yes, you can.

必要表示： must, have to

You must/have to finish the work.

区别： must表示主观看法；have to表示客观需要

二、动词时态

1. 一般现在时 (Simple Present Tense)

表示经常性、习惯性的动作或客观真理。

主语 动词变化
I, we, you, they, 复数名词 动词原形
he, she, it, 单数名词 动词 + s/es

I get up at six every morning.

The earth goes around the sun.

2. 现在进行时 (Present Continuous Tense)

表示说话时正在进行或现阶段正在进行的动作。

结构：am/is/are + 动词-ing

She is having a bath now.

Kate is learning Italian.

3. 一般将来时 (Simple Future Tense)

表示将要发生的动作或存在的状态。

结构：will/shall + 动词原形 或 am/is/are going to + 动词原形

I will leave for Beijing tomorrow.

We are going to hold the Olympic Games.

4. 一般过去时 (Simple Past Tense)

表示过去发生的动作或状态。

结构：动词过去式

I bought a book yesterday.

They weren't able to come.

5. 现在完成时 (Present Perfect Tense)

表示过去发生的动作对现在造成的影响或结果，或从过去持续到现在的动作。

结构：have/has + 过去分词

She has never read this novel.

I have been a teacher for 10 years.

动词时态练习题

请选择下列各题的最佳答案，选择后将显示解析：

1 --What are Mr and Mrs Black doing?
--They ______ tea in the garden.
A. are drinking
B. drank
C. have drunk
D. drink

正确答案：A. are drinking

解析：根据问句的现在进行时态，答句时态要一致，表示正在进行的动作。

2 My mother often asks me ______ early.
A. get up
B. got up
C. getting up
D. to get up

正确答案：D. to get up

解析：根据句中的谓语动词ask的固定搭配 ask sb to do sth。

3 Soon Wu Dong ______ up with Li Lei, then they were neck and neck.
A. taught
B. caught
C. bought
D. brought

正确答案：B. caught

解析：根据固定动词词组 catch up with sb 及第二句的过去时态。

4 The car ______ and stopped at the red traffic light.
A. got on
B. got off
C. slowed down
D. picked up

正确答案：C. slowed down

解析：根据后半句在红灯时停下来，选择"减速"。

5 Tom ______ the CD player for two weeks.
A. has lent
B. has borrowed
C. has bought
D. has had

正确答案：D. has had

解析：此题考查现在完成时的延续性和非延续性动词，根据for two weeks时间短语，需使用延续性动词have。

6 --Do you know ______?
--Sorry, but if he ______ back, I ______ you know as soon as possible.
A. when will he be back, comes, will let
B. when he will be back, will come, will let
C. what time will he be back, will come, let
D. what time he will be back, comes, will let

正确答案：D. what time he will be back, comes, will let

解析：宾语从句要用陈述句语序；if条件句用一般现在时表示将来，主句用一般将来时。

7 We ______ to the park if it is fine tomorrow.
A. will go
B. have gone
C. go

正确答案：A. will go

解析：if条件句的主句要用一般将来时。

8 A new shoe factory will ______ in this part of the city.
A. be building
B. be built
C. build

正确答案：B. be built

解析：工厂是被建造，因此需要使用被动语态。

9 --______ all your things, Tom! I hate them here and there.
--Ok, Mom.
A. Put up
B. Put on
C. Put down
D. Put away

正确答案：D. Put away

解析：put away 意为"把...收拾好"，符合上下文语境。

10 --How about going hiking this weekend?
--Sorry, I prefer ______ rather than ______.
A. to stay at home, go out
B. to go out, stay at home
C. staying at home, go out
D. going out, stay at home

正确答案：A. to stay at home, go out

解析：固定结构为 prefer to do sth rather than do sth。
