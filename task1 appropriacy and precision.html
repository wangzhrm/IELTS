雅思写作 Task 1 词汇指南

提升词汇多样性和准确性

Table, Bar Chart, and Pie Chart (数据类图表)

这类图表的核心是描述数据的变化、对比和趋势。

描述趋势和变化 (Describing Trends and Changes)

  • 上升 (Increases): 动词：soar, rocket, surge, climb, rise, increase；名词：an increase, a climb, a growth, a surge
  • 下降 (Decreases): 动词：plunge, plummet, decline, drop, fall, decrease；名词：a decrease, a decline, a drop, a fall
  • 稳定 (Stability): 动词：level off, stabilise, remain stable, remain constant；名词：a period of stability, a plateau
  • 波动 (Fluctuations): 动词：fluctuate, vary, oscillate；名词：a fluctuation, a variation, an oscillation
  • 程度副词 (Adverbs of Degree): dramatically, sharply, steeply, significantly (显著变化)；gradually, steadily, moderately (平缓变化)；slightly, marginally, minimally (微小变化)

描述对比和比较 (Describing Comparisons)

  • 最高/最低 (Superlatives): the highest figure, the lowest proportion, the largest percentage, the smallest amount
  • 相似/不同 (Similarities/Differences): ...was comparable to...；...was twice as large as...；in contrast, by comparison, while
  • 排名 (Ranking): ranked first, came in second, followed by, outweigh

Map (地图题)

这类图表要求描述一个区域在不同时间点的变化，通常涉及建筑物或基础设施的增减。

描述变化 (Describing Changes)

  • was converted into, was demolished/pulled down, was rebuilt/redeveloped
  • was constructed, was erected, was added
  • remained unchanged, was replaced by

描述位置 (Describing Locations and Positions)

  • to the north/south/east/west of
  • in the centre of, in the corner of
  • adjacent to, next to, opposite, alongside

描述发展 (Describing Development)

  • underwent significant changes, saw a complete transformation, witnessed a dramatic redevelopment

Diagram / Process (流程图)

这类图表要求描述一个产品或过程的步骤，通常是线性的。

描述步骤顺序 (Describing Sequence)

  • The first step is..., Initially..., To begin with...
  • Then..., Next..., Following this..., Subsequently...
  • The next stage involves..., At the same time..., Concurrently...
  • Finally..., The last stage is..., The process concludes with...

描述动作和状态 (Describing Actions and States)

  • is transported to, is transferred to
  • is filtered through, is heated to, is converted into
  • is produced, is manufactured, is collected, is stored
