Reference and Substitution Exercises

Practice using reference and substitution to improve the coherence and cohesion of your writing. For each exercise, rewrite the sentences to make them more natural and less repetitive. Then, click the checkmark to see a possible solution.

Reference Exercises (Pronouns & Demonstratives)

1. The new subway line opened last month. The new subway line has significantly reduced traffic congestion.

✔
Hint: Use a pronoun to refer to 'the new subway line'.
Example Answer: The new subway line opened last month. It has significantly reduced traffic congestion.

2. Sales of smartphones have been declining. This decline has surprised many analysts.

✔
Hint: Use a demonstrative pronoun to refer to the whole idea of the decline.
Example Answer: Sales of smartphones have been declining. This has surprised many analysts.

3. The number of people working from home increased. The increase was due to recent technological advances.

✔
Hint: Use a demonstrative pronoun to link the sentences.
Example Answer: The number of people working from home increased. This was due to recent technological advances.

4. The chart shows the price of oil. The price has been volatile over the past decade.

✔
Hint: Use a pronoun to refer back to 'the price of oil'.
Example Answer: The chart shows the price of oil, which has been volatile over the past decade. (Using a relative pronoun can also work).

5. The two main causes of pollution are cars and factories. Cars and factories are a major concern for the government.

✔
Hint: Use a plural pronoun to replace 'cars and factories'.
Example Answer: The two main causes of pollution are cars and factories. They are a major concern for the government.

Substitution Exercises (Synonyms & General Terms)

1. The graph indicates a steep rise in consumer spending. This steep rise was unexpected.

✔
Hint: Replace 'steep rise' with a synonym or a more general phrase.
Example Answer: The graph indicates a steep rise in consumer spending. This significant increase was unexpected.

2. The number of new housing developments grew. The growth put pressure on local schools.

✔
Hint: Substitute 'growth' with a related term.
Example Answer: The number of new housing developments grew. This expansion put pressure on local schools.

3. There has been a notable decrease in the number of people who smoke. The decrease is a result of public health campaigns.

✔
Hint: Replace the second 'decrease' with a synonym.
Example Answer: There has been a notable decrease in the number of people who smoke. This decline is a result of public health campaigns.

4. The company's profits surged last year. This surge was due to a new product launch.

✔
Hint: Use a different noun to refer to the 'surge'.
Example Answer: The company's profits surged last year. This growth was due to a new product launch.

5. The population of the town remained stable. The stability was maintained by strict zoning laws.

✔
Hint: Use a synonym for 'stability' to avoid repetition.
Example Answer: The population of the town remained stable. This steadiness was maintained by strict zoning laws.
