如何改写题目

使用同义词替换：

  • "shows" → "illustrates", "demonstrates", "presents", "gives information about"
  • "proportion" → "percentage", "share"
  • "number of" → "how many"
  • "from...to..." → "over a period of", "between...and..."

例句：

原题: "The graph shows the proportion of the population aged 65 and over between 1940 and 2040 in three different countries."

改写: "The line graph illustrates the percentage of elderly people aged 65 and above in the USA, Sweden, and Japan over a century from 1940 to 2040."

总体趋势表达

描述总体趋势：

  • Overall, the graph shows...
  • In general, it can be seen that...
  • Broadly speaking, the data indicates...
  • The most striking feature of the graph is...

趋势描述词汇：

  • 上升: increase, rise, grow, climb, soar, rocket
  • 下降: decrease, fall, drop, decline, plunge, plummet
  • 波动: fluctuate, vary, experience fluctuations
  • 稳定: remain stable/steady/unchanged, stabilize, level off

    数据描述技巧

    描述具体数据：

    • 使用准确的数据：from 30% to 50%, reaching a peak of 75%
    • 描述变化幅度：dramatically, significantly, moderately, slightly
    • 描述变化速度：rapidly, gradually, suddenly, steadily

    例句：

    • In 1990, the figure stood at 20%, then it rose dramatically to 45% by 2000.
    • After reaching a peak of 60% in 2010, the number plummeted to just 25% by 2015.
    • Throughout the period, Country A consistently had the highest proportion, averaging around 40%.

    时态和单位

    时态使用：

    • 过去时间：使用过去时 (was, increased, fell)
    • 现在时间：使用现在时 (is, shows, indicates)
    • 未来预测：使用将来时 (will, is projected to, is expected to)

    单位使用：

    • 百分比: percent或% (写作中最好用percent)
    • 数字: 注意千位分隔符
    • 货币: $, £, €等
    • 度量单位: kg, km, m²等

    例句：

    • In 1980, the population was 2.5 million, and it is projected to reach 4 million by 2040.
    • Spending on education increased from $500 million in 2000 to $750 million in 2010.
